Our Verification Process

Trust and transparency are our top priorities. To ensure every donation link on this platform is legitimate and effective, we follow a strict verification protocol.

Vetting Criteria:

  • Official Registration: The organization must be a registered non-governmental organization (NGO), charity, or an official United Nations agency.
  • Global Recognition: We prioritize organizations with a long-standing international presence and recognition from major humanitarian bodies.
  • Financial Transparency: Organizations are checked for publicly available financial reports that demonstrate how funds are allocated.
  • On-the-Ground Presence: The entity must have a verifiable, active presence in Palestine, delivering aid directly to the people in need.
  • Non-Partisan Status: We only list organizations that are strictly humanitarian and not affiliated with any political or militant groups.

This process helps ensure that your support goes directly to providing humanitarian, medical, and educational aid to Palestinians.
